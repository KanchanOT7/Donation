About us

DONATE is a platform which exists to help cancer patients get their treatment. Established in 1999, it is the largest and the most trusted platform in India. It is easy to use and conveniently collects the funds from the donars. The collected money is then given to the needful cancer patient though different organisations. DONATE has now become a community of 2M+ donars and 200+ partners, serving 10M+ people across the country.
